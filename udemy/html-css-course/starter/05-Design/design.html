Typography(legibility, readability and Appealability) Rules

Rules

  • use good & popular typefaces be it serif/sans-serif. use 1/2 typefaces at max. choice depends on website personality
  • use a font-size for normal text - 16px to 32px
  • long text format (blog post) - 20px or more.
  • headlines - 50px or more and font-weight 600+ depending on the personality
  • for any text don't use a font-weight < 400
  • use less than 75 chars per line
  • use line-height between 1.5-2 and less than 1.5 for large text
  • decrease letter spacing in headlines
  • ALL CAPS for short titles. Make them small and bold.
  • Dont center long text blocks.
